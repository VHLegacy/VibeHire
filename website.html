How It Works

01

We start with a short brief about your business, your goals, your audience, and the pages or sections you need. Not sure what you need? That's fine — we'll guide you through it and make recommendations based on what works for businesses like yours.

We produce 2 design mockups — desktop and mobile view — for you to choose from. These are real designs, not rough sketches. Once you pick one, we lock it in and move into full development. Changes after this point follow the revision policy.

FAQ

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know about our website development service.

Why custom code instead of WordPress? +
WordPress is great for DIY — but that's also the problem. Anyone can use the same template, break things with a bad plugin, or get hacked through an outdated theme. Custom HTML/CSS means your site is clean, fast, fully yours, and you don't depend on third-party systems to keep it running. You also rely on us for updates, which means you have a long-term partner, not a one-off job.
What is a "mockup"? +
A mockup is a visual design of your website — what it will look like before we build it. We produce 2 different design directions for you to choose from, both in desktop and mobile view. You pick the one you prefer, give us feedback, and then we build from that approved design.
What do I need to provide? +
At minimum, your logo, brand colours, and any images you want on the site. If you don't have content written yet, our Content Writing add-on covers that. If you don't have a logo yet, let us know — we can advise on the right next step.
Do you handle hosting and domain? +
We assist with domain setup at no extra charge. Hosting is handled separately — we'll recommend a reliable, affordable option based on your needs. Hosting and domain fees go directly to the provider, not to us.
What if I need more than 3 pages? +
Each additional page or section starts from RM200. We'll quote the exact amount based on your requirements before any extra work begins. No surprises.
How long does it take? +
Our standard timeline is 20 working days: 3 days for brief and direction, 4 days for mockups, 2 days for design selection, 8 days for development, and 3 days for final review and handover. Timelines may be affected by how quickly you respond and approve at each stage.
Who owns the website after it's done? +
You do — completely. Full source code is handed over upon final payment. No ongoing fees to VibeHire, no lock-in. The site is entirely yours to host, modify, or hand to another developer in the future.
