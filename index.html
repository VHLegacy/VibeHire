How It Works · AI Video Production

Easy Steps.
No Guesswork.

We made the process as simple as possible. You give us the brief — we handle the rest, start to finish.

01

Send Us Your Brief — Using AI

You don't need to fill in any forms. Just copy the prompt below and paste it into ChatGPT, Gemini, or any AI tool you already use. The AI will ask you a few simple questions about your business, then generate a ready-made company brief for you. Once it's done, just send it to us and we'll take it from there.

📋 Copy This Prompt
I need your help creating a company brief that I can send to a video production agency. Start by asking me a few short questions to understand my business better — things like what I do, who my customers are, what I want to achieve, and how I want to come across. Keep the questions simple and conversational, one or two at a time. Once you have enough information, generate a structured company brief that covers: what the business does, who the target audience is, the brand tone and personality, the main goal for the video, preferred platforms, and any key messages or upcoming announcements. The brief should be clear enough for a video production team to understand my business and start creating the right content — without needing to ask me too many more questions.
02

We Take It From Here

Once we have your brief, our team gets to work. We'll confirm the content direction with you first, then move into storyboarding, scripting, AI narration, and video editing — keeping you updated at each stage so there are no surprises.

03

Your Video Is Ready to Post

You receive a polished, professional video — fully edited and ready to upload to Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or wherever your audience is. The video is 100% yours once final payment is made.

FAQ

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know before getting started — straight to the point, no fluff.

Do I need to provide a script or any content? +
No. All you need to do is fill in our AI brief prompt — it takes less than 5 minutes. We handle the storyboard, script, narration, and editing from there. You just review and approve.
What kind of videos do you produce? +
We produce short-form videos (under 1 minute) for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and Facebook — as well as longer videos (1–3 minutes) for YouTube and websites. All videos include AI narration and professional editing.
How long does it take to get my video? +
Our standard turnaround is 10 working days: 3 days for content confirmation, 2 days for storyboard and scripting, 2 days for video generation, and 3 days for final editing and delivery. Rush orders may be accommodated — contact us to check availability.
Who owns the video after it's done? +
You do. Full copyright and ownership is transferred to you upon final payment. You can use the video on any platform, for any purpose, without restriction. We may use it in our portfolio unless you request otherwise.
What if I'm not happy with the result? +
Every video includes 2 rounds of revisions — one after the storyboard/script stage, and one after the final edit. We want you to be happy with the output. Additional revision rounds are available at RM100 each.
Do you offer Bahasa Malaysia narration? +
Yes. We offer both Bahasa Malaysia and English AI narration. Bilingual (mixed BM + English) narration is also available — just let us know your preference in the brief.
Can I cancel a monthly package? +
Monthly packages run per billing cycle. You can cancel before the next cycle begins. Unused videos within a cycle do not roll over. We recommend the per-video option if you're not sure about volume yet.
How do I get started? +
Simple — tap the WhatsApp button anywhere on this page, say hi, and we'll walk you through the next steps. No long forms, no contracts upfront. Just a quick chat to understand your needs.
